About Chit Fund Calculator

This financial tool by Vazga Valamudan helps users in Kallipatti, Gobi, and across Tamil Nadu manage their chit fund investments. It calculates the monthly dividend savings, auction discounts, and provides a clear strategy on whether taking the chit now acts as a low-interest loan or a high-return investment.

How to use the Chit Fund Calculator?

Enter your total chit amount, number of installments, and the current auction discount. The tool will instantly show you how much you receive after commission and your per-installment savings.
